Firestorm from Earth X

You are Firestorm from Earth X.



Your alignment is Neutral Killing.

Unlike your doppelganger on Earth-1, you use your powers for evil, seeking to burn the town to ashes, killing all of its residents in the process.

Each night you may douse one player or ignite all of your doused targets.
If you are doused by another Arsonist, you may wait a night to clean the gasoline off of yourself.
Your method of killing ignores night immunity.
You cover up your tracks very well and you will appear as 'not suspicious' to a Sheriff.
You are unkillable at night. If somebody tries to attack you, they will receive a message saying that you were immune.
You win if only you and Ra's al Ghul or Clifford DeVoe remain.

**Note that this flavor particularly does not accurately portray Crisis on Earth X, I just needed something that fit**

Deadshot

You are a Deadshot.



Your alignment is Neutral Killing.

A highly skilled assassin that is known to never miss and is extremely knowledgeable about various types of weapons. Now you have decided to focus on using explosives.

Each night you may place a device on a player; they will be informed of this.
If a player with a device visits another player with a device, all devices will explode and kill the player holding them. This ignores night immunity.
You may plant a device on yourself; however, visiting a target while you have a device won't trigger them unless the device on the other player is from a previous night.
You are immune to your explosions.
You are redirection immune.
You are night immune.

You win with Deadshot
You win with Savitar
You win with Amanda Waller
